M.e.e.t.i.n.g.p.o.i.n.t. Issue 3

This is an exchange room. Beyond this page, on a server, lies the content of the third M.e.e.t.i.n.g.p.o.i.n.t. publication: “In the institute”, a song and video composition by Hrefna Sigurðardóttir.

Although we do not ask for any type of monetary exchange, instead, we’d like you to submit [?] a single file of your own choice in exchange for the mp4 publication. So! At the moment you are nowhere. You are situated in plasma, in goo – in limbo. Not much to do here but decide which file you’d like to submit. What size, what type, which genre...or if you’d like to submit one at all.

You, the visitor, may be coming to this room from different operating systems and not all of you are looking through the same type of browser or screen. This may mean that the file extensions hosted on your computer are different, and the type of file may differ as well. Maybe you are interested or invested in vastly different subjects which probably also controls what kind of files you have saved on your machine.

And this might result in a quite eclectic collection of digital objects. But that is somewhat what we are trying to create – a collection. Just some collection that we will later on re-order, re-frame and re-publish, with your consent, of course.


AND if this page is plasma, the buttons above then act as the membrane that sucks in or spits out the documents exchanged.

As you upload, add your name and a title if you’d like.

We wonder how you will choose your file...

In the institute, song and video composition, intitally performed live in 2018 by Hrefna Sigurðardóttir at Partners, New Haven.
